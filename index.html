About

MIMUC is a friendly, student-led mathematics conference for undergraduates across pure, applied, and statistics. Talks are short, posters are welcome, and first-time presenters are encouraged.

Venue

The Alan Turing Building, University of Manchester. Fully accessible with step-free access and a nearby quiet room.
